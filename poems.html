Victoria Jean Reynolds

Poems

Victoria has written poetry since a very young age, according to her mother in a recorded interview in January of 2002. These poems have always originated from a feeling of loss.

Interviews

After being published by Sunset Liminal Press two editions in a row, head editor, Greg Scheiber, chose to interview Victoria to ask about inspiration and influences, her writing process, as well as the personal, confessional nature of her poems.

The interview outlines some of her poetic influences, like Edgar Allan Poe and Marianne Moore. She speaks in further depth about Alison Townsend and her book, Persephone in America and the impression it made on her.

Despite the depressing topics of many of her poems, in this interview, Victoria insists that she is "great at parties."

Interview conducted by Greg Scheiber, head editor at Sunset Liminal in November of 2015.
