Victoria Jean Reynolds

Background

Victoria Jean Reynolds is a writer and poet from Flourtown, Pennsylvania. She received her BA in English Literature with a concentration in Creative Writing from Gettysburg College and is in the process of earning her MFA in poetry at George Mason University. After being assigned Persephone in America by Alison Townsend in her ENGH 302: Free Verse & Form in Poetry class, Victoria made a decision to pursue her senior honors thesis the following fall at Gettysburg College as a collection of poetry titled, Fiori e Febbre, translated as "Flowers and Fever" from Italian.
